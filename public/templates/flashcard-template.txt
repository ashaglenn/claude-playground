CARD 3

BACK: The movement of water molecules through a semi-permeable membrane from an area of low solute concentration to high solute concentration.

CARD 4
FRONT: What is the formula for the area of a circle?
BACK: A = πr² (pi times radius squared)

---
NOTES:
- Each card starts with "CARD #" followed by FRONT: and BACK: fields
- TITLE and DESCRIPTION at the top are optional
- You can have as many cards as you want
- Add FRONT_IMAGE: or BACK_IMAGE: with a URL for images (text will be on left, image on right)
- Images can also be uploaded directly in the builder after importing

Alternative formats also work:

Simple format (separated by blank lines):
Q: What is the capital of France?
A: Paris

Tab-separated format (one card per line):
Term	Definition
Vocabulary Word	Its meaning

Dash format (simple terms):
Photosynthesis - The process plants use to make food from sunlight
